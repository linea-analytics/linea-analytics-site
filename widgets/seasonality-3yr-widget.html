The 2-Year Ambiguity: Why More Data Matters

Both years show the same pattern: December spend goes up, December revenue goes up. But does the spend cause the uplift, or is December simply a strong month every year?

Both interpretations fit:

With 2 years of correlated spend and revenue, there is no way to tell which is right.

Dec 2024: £141,300 — zero media spend.

December spiked again, without a single pound of spend. This rules out Theory B and confirms: the uplift is seasonal.

Years 1 and 2 were even higher because seasonality and media spend added together. A third year — with no spend — finally reveals the baseline seasonal pattern.
